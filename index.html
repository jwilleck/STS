About Us

The Success Through Scriptures (STS) ministry is an organization of outdoor enthusiasts who love to fish, hunt, canoe, snowmobile and camp. We, by personal experience, have learned that only through God and His Word is any true success possible. Our purpose is to mentor men and boys in Godly wisdom through the scriptures and outdoor adventures.

STS is affiliated with Westwood Community Church.
